Wild beaches, nature and aquamarine waters

Al Hoceima is famous for its secluded beaches with clear turquoise waters bordered by rocks. Whether in Quemado, or Cala Bonita, or Sfiha, enjoy the golden fine sand of these heavenly beaches, and the warmth of the Mediterranean climate.

Al Hoceima is not only a simple seaside town. Its surroundings has a a lots of treasures that adventure lovers come in droves to explore. Outside the town, near the fishing village of Torres de Alcala, are the ruins of five watchtowers. The Portuguese had built them during their colony.

Located in the south of Morocco, Dakhla is a small part of paradise, lost between the waters of the Atlantic and the sands of the Sahara. It gives you a complete change of scenery

It's not a surprise that Kitesurfers from all over the wolrd come in droves to this paradisiacal destination : crystalline waters, fine sand, a Caribbean scenery, its shores has one of the flattest water spots in the world.Going down further south, you’ll arrive to the superb white sand beach of Puertito, as well as the beach of Puerto Rico, where you’ll have the opportunity to sleep in tents beneath the stars.
